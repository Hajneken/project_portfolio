Kandy is an acient city and one of the most visited destinations in Sri Lanka 🇱🇰. It is a sacred place where Budha's tooth is treasured in the Sri Dalada Maligawa or the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, located in a marvelous city center next to a lake. For the most part, the city hosts one of the oldest festivals in the world. The festival is called Kandy Esala Perahera and takes place for 2 weeks, gradually increasing in size each day. I have to confess that I have never seen so many elephants in one place. That is illustrated by couple of hundred decorated elephants that travel from all corners of the island to participate in this holy procession. According to the legend, the first settler, a man of Viking origin, buried a treasure in a cave behind the waterfall. Being one of the biggest in the country, the continuous mass of water prevents anyone from retrieving the treasure. Believe it or not, the fact remains that this majestic waterfall is the last one on the Skóga River, concluding series of at least 33 hefty waterfalls over the distance of 8 km. Unfortunately, while venturing couple of meters up stream and witnessing one more, I only learned this fact after returning home. I might repeat myself, nonetheless, the reality is that this fact gives me 33 more reasons to return to magical Iceland 🇮🇸. #skógafoss #skogafoss #Iceland #Icelandic #waterfall #magical #landscape #landscapephotography #amazing #nature #story #storytime #storytelling #breathtaking #breathtakingviews #stunning #river #naturephotography
